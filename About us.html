About Us

This here on the left image is Richard Patterson or (Rich), is the CEO & CTO of Trion Supercars, where he realized a lifelong dream in May 2012 after 40 years in the automotive industry by founding Trion with a core team of experts.
His passion for cars started in his Detroit childhood, then at 15, he earned a GM apprenticeship after winning a statewide drafting contest. A graduate of Mumford High School and Hall of Fame inductee, Rich have began his career
as a CAD designer at Modern Engineering, quickly rising to leadership roles due to his expertise in automotive interior systems. With over two decades of experience, including contributions to Chrysler's Stow 'n Go seating system, Rich
moved to the West Coast to immerse himself in design innovation. Today, he brings his vision to life with Trion Supercars, offering high-performance American-made supercars that combine style, speeds of 300 mph, and comfort, with options
ranging from traditional engines to hybrid and fully electric models. On the bottom left this image is the Trion team where they are the experts whom create, design, and make the cars of Trion while bottom roight is one of their products.
What sets Trion Supercars apart its their cutting-edge approach to blend to the extreme performance with advanced technology and luxury. Trion pushes the limits of innovation by offering electric and hybrid powertrains that deliver impressive
speed and acceleration, rivaling traditional supercars. The brand also leads the way in automotive design, with futuristic features like a fully digital cockpit and wireless charging. In addition to performance, Trion is committed to sustainability,
using eco-friendly materials without compromising on quality. The combination of power, high-tech connectivity, and a personalized driving experience sets Trion apart, positioning it as a standout in the competitive supercar market.
